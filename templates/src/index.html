Frequently asked questions

What is the purpose of this web app?

This web app is designed to help users assess the risk of prepayment for mortgage loans. By entering various loan and borrower details, the app provides insights into the likelihood of prepayment based on historical data.

How does the app calculate prepayment risk?

The app uses a regression algorithm that takes into account factors such as loan amount, interest rate, credit score, loan term, and more. It then predicts the likelihood of prepayment based on these inputs.

How accurate are the predictions ?

The accuracy of predictions can vary based on the quality of the input data and the model's performance. It's important to note that this app provides estimates and should not be considered financial advice.

What is prepayment risk?

Prepayment risk refers to the possibility that borrowers will pay off their mortgage loans earlier than their scheduled payment dates. This can impact lenders and investors, leading to potential changes in expected cash flows.

Find out more information by reading the license.

Can I use this app for different types of mortgages?

No, the app can be used Only four fixed-rate mortgages

Can I trust the predictions provided by the app?

The predictions are generated using historical data and a regression algorithm. While the app aims to provide accurate estimates, it's important to understand that predictions may not account for all variables that can influence prepayment behavior.

Are there any fees associated with using this app?

No, using this web app is free of charge. It's designed to provide users with valuable insights into prepayment risk without any costs.

What if my loan information changes after I've used the app?

If your loan information changes (e.g., interest rate adjustment, loan modification), it's a good idea to re-evaluate prepayment risk using the updated details for a more accurate assessment.
